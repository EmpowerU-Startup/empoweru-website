Episode 7: Hugo Rodas speaks on UCLA, overcoming adversity, teaching and More

In this week's episode, we have Hugo Rodas. Hugo is a senior at UCLA studying history with plans to graduate by the Winter quarter of 2022. After his undergraduate studies, Hugo's goal is to attend UCLA’s Teacher Education Program (TEP) to pursue a master's degree in education and earn his teaching credential concurrently. Hugo is currently working at LA’s Best with elementary kids after school to teach them sports and chess.
